What Are Programming Languages?

Programming languages are tools used to instruct computers to perform specific tasks. They enable developers to create software, applications, websites, and much more. Each language is unique in its structure and functionality, allowing for specialized use cases.

On this platform, we explore three major categories: Structured/Procedural Languages, Object-Oriented Languages, and Frontend Technologies. These are key pillars of modern programming, each offering distinct features and benefits.
